About AudioLens

AudioLens are assistive technology prescription glasses designed for vision impaired individuals – when worn it feeds them audio of what is in front of them, essentially becoming their eyes. It is an AI/ML device that can read the users real time environment that aids in users' day-to-day life, which includes, but is not limited to weather detection, detection of fixed/future obstructions, facial recognition, read text upon request, etc.,
